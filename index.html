Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account and all associated data from the Scarecrow System, follow the steps below:

  • Log in to the AgriCrow App using your account credentials.
  • Go to Settings and select the Delete Account option.
  • Confirm your request to delete the account by following the on-screen instructions.

Once your request is submitted, your account and data will be permanently removed within 7 days.
